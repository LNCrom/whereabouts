# Whereabouts Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Whereabouts is a personal family location dashboard built around consent and member-controlled sharing.

## Data Collection

The current Whereabouts build does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the developer or any third-party service.

The app currently uses sample family/location data inside the app experience. Contact selection, when used, is intended to help you choose people for your local family circle experience. The current build does not upload contacts, location data, identifiers, or usage data to a server.

## Location Data

Whereabouts is designed for consent-based family location sharing. The current build does not provide hidden tracking and does not access private Apple Find My location data. Apple does not make Find My people-location data available to third-party apps.

If future versions add real location sharing, sharing will require permission from the person sharing their location, and this policy will be updated before release.

## Children and Families

Whereabouts is intended for personal family coordination. The current build does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
